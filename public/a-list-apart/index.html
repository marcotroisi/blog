10 Nov '12 A List Apart

I recently found a very interesting and relevant blog/magazine for web developers: A List Apart.

“A List Apart Magazine… explores the design, development, and meaning of web content, with a special focus on web standards and best practices.” (from http://www.alistapart.com/about/).

It seems to be a very well written magazine, and there are more than a few interesting articles, in my opinion. Not surprisingly, what I like more are the articles about the responsive design best practices.

I believe this is one of the bookmarks every web developer should add to his browser!
