Writing for Milk & Honey Mag

I have recently written a couple of articles for Milk & Honey Mag, which is a lifestyle magazine for women.

The articles that I have written for them so far are:

  • Chromebook from Google: smaller than a laptop, more useful than a tablet

    Here I try to give an overview of what a Chromebook is, and what are the differences between it and the devices that everyone already knows (laptops, tablets, etc.).

    Since I am addressing an audience composed mostly by women, I am also focussing on the “cool factor”, as it seems to me that for a tech product to succeed, having the best combination of software and hardware put together is just not enough; for a product to become a must-have, a great design and a solid, recognisable brand are also necessary.

  • Scribd is like Netflix for ebooks

    A quick introduction and overview of [Scribd][5], a new, interesting service that allows you to read millions of ebooks just by paying a small monthly subscription.

I hope you will enjoy reading these articles. Let me know what do you think about them, I’d be more than happy to hear from you!
